Open Source Political Intelligence

This project aimes to help organizes the aggregation and analysis of political news. The vision is a set of tools to provide insights into political events and trends. Helping to understand the political landscape and the forces that shape it. No bias, but clear methods with reproducible and transparent results. Built on top of many sources, with open source code and an idealistic spirit to help people understand the world around them. A toolkit for your friends, researchers and the Christmas Dinner evening discussions alike.

Your skills and enthusiasm can ignite change. Join us as a data expert, activist, sponsor or curious individual. Political intelligence is a team sport. And true insight comes from many perspectives.
